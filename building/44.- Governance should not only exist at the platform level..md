## Section 6.1 — Purpose

Governance establishes the constitutional framework through which every Living Story Universe operates.

Its purpose is to ensure that creative evolution remains:

- trustworthy
- transparent
- accountable
- equitable
- traceable
- sustainable

Governance protects the interests of all legitimate participants while preserving the integrity of every Living Story Universe.

No creative ecosystem can scale without governance.

Governance exists to enable freedom through clearly defined responsibilities rather than unrestricted control.

# CTO Recommendation (Major Architectural Evolution)

After writing this article, I believe we've reached another important realization.

**Governance should not only exist at the platform level.**

It should exist at **three constitutional layers**:

```
Platform Governance
        │
        ▼
Story Universe Governance
        │
        ▼
Creative Session Governance
```

### 1. Platform Governance

Applies to the entire byDebut ecosystem:

* constitutional principles
* AI ethics
* global policies
* trust framework

### 2. Story Universe Governance

Defined by each Story Passport:

* who may contribute
* chronology rules
* editorial workflow
* canon approval
* licensing

### 3. Creative Session Governance

Temporary rules for a specific collaboration:

* invited contributors
* AI permissions for that session
* review requirements
* session deadlines
* publication workflow

This three-layer governance model is far more scalable than a single governance system and will support everything from individual authors to large publishing houses, universities, research institutions, and future enterprise customers without redesigning the platform. I recommend capturing this as an Architecture Decision Record (ADR) and later reflecting it in the Domain Model and Supabase schema. I believe it will become one of the defining architectural characteristics of byDebut.
