# byDebut Company Constitution v1.0

---

## Article I

### Why We Exist

byDebut exists to transform stories from static works into living creative ecosystems.

Every decision must increase the long-term value of both the original work and the creative community that grows around it.

If a decision weakens either one, it is the wrong decision.

---

## Article II

### We Protect Original Creativity

Original authors are never replaced.

Original works are never diminished.

Original rights are always respected.

Everything begins with the Story Passport.

---

## Article III

### Communities Create Value

Readers are not passive consumers.

Readers become participants.

Participants become creators.

Creators strengthen the Story Universe.

Every community should leave a story richer than it found it.

---

## Article IV

### AI Exists to Amplify Human Creativity

Artificial Intelligence is infrastructure.

Human imagination is the source.

AI should never become the protagonist.

Humans remain the authors of meaning.

---

## Article V

### Every Story Universe Is Independent

Every Story Passport defines its own rules.

Every Rights Holder defines participation.

There is no single publishing model.

There are millions of Story Constitutions.

---

## Article VI

### Trust Is More Valuable Than Growth

We will never sacrifice trust for short-term metrics.

Rights.

Transparency.

Traceability.

Attribution.

These are permanent.

---

## Article VII

### Simplicity Wins

Every feature should answer one question:

**Does this help someone tell, continue, discover, or protect a story?**

If not, reconsider it.

---

## Article VIII

### Every Decision Must Strengthen the Flywheel

Every feature should improve at least one of:

* Rights Holder Success
* Reader Participation
* Community Growth
* Story Intelligence
* Story Passport Value
* Lineage Quality

If it improves none, it does not belong.

---

## Article IX

### We Build Platforms, Not Features

Features disappear.

Platforms evolve.

Every new capability should strengthen the platform rather than exist in isolation.

---

## Article X

### We Build for Decades

We are not building software for a hackathon.

We are building infrastructure for the future of storytelling.

The platform should still make sense twenty years from now.
