Technology alone will not make byDebut successful.

# The Four Pillars of the Ecosystem

The platform operates on four interconnected pillars.

```text
                RIGHTS

                   │

                   ▼

          STORY PASSPORTS

                   │

                   ▼

         CREATIVE ECONOMY

                   │

                   ▼

        COMMUNITY INTELLIGENCE
```

None of these pillars can exist independently.

---

# Pillar 1 — Rights Governance

Everything begins with trust.

No story enters the ecosystem without authorization.

Every Story Passport must have a verified Rights Holder.

Every Story Lineage inherits the permissions defined by that Rights Holder.

Rights are never assumed.

They are explicitly defined.

---

## Rights Holder Control

Every Rights Holder decides:

* Who may create Lineages.
* Whether AI-generated content is allowed.
* Whether commercial derivative works are permitted.
* Whether educational adaptations are allowed.
* Whether producer access is enabled.
* Whether community voting is enabled.
* Whether public discovery is enabled.

The Rights Holder controls the universe.

byDebut provides the infrastructure.

---

# Pillar 2 — Story Governance

Every Living Story Universe has its own operating rules.

These rules are stored inside the Story Passport.

Examples:

* Canonical characters
* Protected characters
* Immutable historical events
* Timeline constraints
* Style constraints
* AI creativity boundaries
* Community participation rules

These become the Story Constitution.

---

# Pillar 3 — Community Governance

Communities evolve naturally.

Chaos should not.

Community governance operates through progressive trust.

### Level 1 — Reader

Can:

* Read
* Comment
* Explore

Cannot:

* Publish public Lineages

---

### Level 2 — Creator

Can:

* Publish Lineages
* Receive feedback
* Build a reputation

---

### Level 3 — Trusted Creator

Can:

* Moderate discussions
* Curate collections
* Mentor new creators

---

### Level 4 — Community Curator

Can:

* Recommend featured Lineages
* Organize community events
* Coordinate Story Challenges

---

### Level 5 — Rights Holder Team

Final authority.

Can approve:

* Canonical Lineages
* Featured adaptations
* Commercial opportunities

Trust is earned through participation.

Not purchased.

---

# Pillar 4 — AI Governance

AI never replaces human authority.

AI proposes.

Humans decide.

The AI Editorial Organization performs:

* analysis,
* planning,
* generation,
* review,
* editing,
* recommendations.

The Rights Holder retains editorial control.
