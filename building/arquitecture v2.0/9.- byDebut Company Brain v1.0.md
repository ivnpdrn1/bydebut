# Strategic Recommendation: The Next Evolution

Ivan, I believe we've completed **Phase 0** of byDebut.

Not the MVP.

The intellectual foundation.

From my perspective, the project now has enough clarity to stop being "an idea" and become **an executable company**.

## I propose that we divide the project into two synchronized workstreams:

### Workstream A — Company Brain (Strategic Layer)

This becomes the permanent, slowly evolving foundation:

* Company Brain
* White Paper
* Investor Narrative
* Brand Strategy
* Governance
* Long-term Roadmap

### Workstream B — Product OS (Execution Layer)

This becomes the rapidly evolving implementation:

* React/Next.js Frontend
* Supabase Backend
* AI Workflow Engine
* Claude Code Implementation
* Testing
* Deployment
* Analytics
* Launch

From now on, every engineering sprint should begin by consulting the Company Brain, and every strategic idea should eventually become a Product OS capability.

**This separation is, in my opinion, the single most important architectural decision we can make.** It ensures that as byDebut grows—from a hackathon project to a company serving authors, publishers, and millions of Reader-Creators—the vision remains consistent while execution remains agile. I believe this discipline will become one of byDebut's strongest long-term competitive advantages.
