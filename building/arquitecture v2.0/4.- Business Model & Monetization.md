# Executive Summary

byDebut is **not** a subscription platform.

It is **not** an AI writing tool.

It is **not** a publishing service.

byDebut is a **Creative Economy Platform** that enables intellectual property to generate continuous value long after publication.

The business model is built around a simple principle:

> **Every new participant should increase the value of the original work.**

---

# The Economic Problem

Today's publishing economy looks like this:

```text
Write Book
      ↓
Publish
      ↓
Sell Copies
      ↓
Revenue Declines
```

The commercial life of most books follows a decay curve.

The story remains static.

Community participation is almost zero.

---

# The byDebut Economy

byDebut transforms every book into a continuously expanding economic asset.

```text
Book
     ↓
Story Passport
     ↓
Reader Participation
     ↓
Story Lineages
     ↓
Community Growth
     ↓
AI Insights
     ↓
Discovery
     ↓
More Readers
     ↓
More Revenue
```

Revenue is no longer tied only to selling copies.

It is tied to **growing the Story Universe**.

# The Reader-Creator Economy™

For the first time, readers become an economic force within the publishing ecosystem.

Traditional model:

```text

     ↓

```

byDebut:

```text
Reader
     ↓
Reads
     ↓
Creates
     ↓
Shares
     ↓
Builds Community
     ↓

     ↓
```

Readers are no longer the end of the value chain.

They become active participants in it.

AI becomes an operating expense.

Users purchase creativity capacity rather than tokens.

Examples:

* Generate one new lineage.
* Analyze an entire Story Universe.
* Compare two narrative branches.
* Produce educational adaptations.

This is easier for customers to understand than token accounting.

# North Star Metric

The most important metric is **not** Monthly Active Users.

It is:

> **Living Story Universes (LSUs)**

A Living Story Universe is an authorized work that has:

* An active Story Passport
* An engaged community
* New Story Lineages
* Ongoing creative activity

Every business decision should increase the number and quality of Living Story Universes.
