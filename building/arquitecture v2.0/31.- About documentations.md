instead of putting **Business** before **Standards**.

The reason is architectural:

* **Standards** define *how* the platform is built (identity, Story Passport, lineage, attribution, coding principles, governance rules).
* **Business** defines *how the platform earns money*.

An engineer should first understand the platform's immutable standards and only then its monetization and commercial strategy. This separation makes it less likely that implementation decisions are driven by business convenience at the expense of core platform principles.
