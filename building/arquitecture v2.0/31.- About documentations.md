## I would make one additional improvement

Looking at everything you've built over the past months, I think we can evolve this from a **Company Brain** into something even more valuable:

**byDebut Knowledge Operating System (byDebut KOS).**

The repository would become much more than documentation. It would be the operational knowledge base that drives Claude Code. Every architectural decision, business rule, UX principle, AI policy, and coding standard would be traceable through a well-defined chain of authority.

This is closer to how large engineering organizations operate: they don't just have documentation—they have a governed knowledge system that every engineer and every AI agent follows.

I recommend we build the repository to that standard from the beginning. It will make byDebut significantly easier to maintain as it grows and will provide Claude Code with a much stronger foundation for consistent implementation.
