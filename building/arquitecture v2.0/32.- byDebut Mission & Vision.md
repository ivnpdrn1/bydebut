# Purpose

This document defines the strategic direction of byDebut.

While the Constitution defines the immutable principles of the company, the Mission & Vision define where the company is going and what success ultimately looks like.

Every future initiative should contribute to achieving this vision.

---

# Mission

Transform every authorized story into a Living Story Universe where human creativity and Artificial Intelligence collaborate to expand narratives while preserving attribution, trust, lineage, and intellectual property.

byDebut empowers stories to continue evolving instead of ending at publication.

---

# Vision

Create the global infrastructure where stories become living ecosystems.

Instead of publishing static books, humanity will publish evolving universes.

Readers become creators.

Creators become collaborators.

Artificial Intelligence becomes an accelerator of creativity rather than a replacement for human imagination.

Every story becomes a continuously expanding knowledge network.

---

# Long-Term Vision

The long-term objective of byDebut is to become the operating system for collaborative storytelling worldwide.

The platform should become the reference infrastructure used by:

- Authors
- Publishers
- Readers
- Universities
- Research organizations
- Educational institutions
- Creative studios
- Entertainment companies
- AI developers

---

# Strategic Objectives

## 1. Build Trust

Every contribution must preserve attribution.

Every participant must be recognized.

Every derivative work must remain traceable.

---

## 2. Create a New Economic Model

Stories should generate value continuously.

Instead of a single publication event, every authorized contribution creates new opportunities for creators.

The ecosystem rewards collaboration instead of replacement.

---

## 3. Build Living Story Universes

Stories are no longer static products.

Every authorized version enriches the original universe.

Knowledge accumulates over time instead of fragmenting.

---

## 4. Human-Centered AI

Artificial Intelligence assists.

Humans decide.

AI expands creativity.

Humans preserve identity.

---

## 5. Global Accessibility

Every language.

Every culture.

Every age group.

Every educational level.

Every creative discipline.

---

# Success Definition

byDebut succeeds when:

- millions of stories continue evolving;
- creators are rewarded for collaboration;
- readers become contributors;
- publishers gain new business opportunities;
- AI strengthens human creativity;
- attribution becomes automatic;
- version history becomes permanent;
- intellectual property becomes transparent and traceable.

---

# Strategic Principles

Every future initiative should answer "Yes" to these questions:

- Does it increase collaboration?
- Does it preserve attribution?
- Does it strengthen trust?
- Does it expand creativity?
- Does it preserve story lineage?
- Does it create long-term value?
- Does it reinforce the ecosystem?

If the answer is "No" to any of these questions, the initiative should be reconsidered.

---

# Relationship to Other Documents

This document defines the destination.
