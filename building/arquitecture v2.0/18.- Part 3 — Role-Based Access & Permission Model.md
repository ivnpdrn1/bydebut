# byDebut Functional Specification v1.0

# Part 3 — Role-Based Access & Permission Model

**Version:** 1.0

---

# 1. Purpose

This document defines every role that exists inside the byDebut ecosystem and exactly what each role can and cannot do.

This is not merely a security model.

It defines how the Living Story Economy™ operates.

Every permission exists to protect intellectual property while maximizing creative participation.

---

# 2. Permission Philosophy

Traditional publishing separates creators from readers.

byDebut intentionally connects them.

However,

participation never means ownership.

Ownership never eliminates collaboration.

AI never replaces human ownership.

Every permission must preserve this balance.

---

# 3. Permission Hierarchy

The ecosystem is organized into seven layers.

```text
Platform Administration

↓

Rights Holder Organizations

↓

Story Universe Owners

↓

Editorial Teams

↓

Professional Contributors

↓

Reader-Creators

↓

Visitors
```

Authority always flows downward.

Creativity flows upward.

# 22. Fundamental Rule

No permission may violate the Story Passport.

The Story Passport remains the immutable source of:

* authorship
* ownership
* licensing
* attribution
* Story DNA
* canonical lineage
* commercial rights

Everything else can evolve.

The Story Passport does not.

---

# 23. Design Principle

The permission system should encourage the widest possible creative participation while preserving legal certainty.

In other words:

* **Readers** become creators.
* **Creators** become recognized contributors.
* **Authors** remain the source of the original work.
* **Publishers** grow living story ecosystems.
* **AI** amplifies human creativity but never acquires authorship or ownership.

This balance is what differentiates byDebut from traditional publishing platforms, AI writing tools, and social content networks.
