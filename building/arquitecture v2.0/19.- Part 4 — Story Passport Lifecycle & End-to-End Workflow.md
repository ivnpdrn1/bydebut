# 1. Purpose

This document defines the complete operational lifecycle of every Story Universe inside byDebut.

It describes every major event from the moment an intellectual property owner decides to join the platform until the story evolves into a self-sustaining Living Story Economy™.

This workflow is the operational heart of byDebut.

Traditional publishing follows this lifecycle:

The objective is not to sell one book.

# 23. Fundamental Principle

A Story Passport is never considered "finished."

It is considered **alive**.

Every authorized contribution extends its creative lifespan, enriches its community, and increases its long-term cultural and commercial value.

The goal of byDebut is not to publish books.

The goal is to cultivate **living story ecosystems** that continue evolving for years or even decades after their original publication.

---

# 24. Strategic Observation

This lifecycle reveals what may become byDebut's strongest competitive advantage.

Traditional publishers monetize the **first publication**.

Social platforms monetize the **audience**.

AI writing tools monetize the **generation of text**.

**byDebut monetizes the continuous evolution of intellectual property while preserving authorship, attribution, and rights.**

That is a fundamentally different economic model, and it is the operational foundation of the **Living Story Economy™**.
