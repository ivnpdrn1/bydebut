Me parece una excelente observación, y creo que no solo debe "tenerse en cuenta": **debe convertirse en uno de los principios arquitectónicos de byDebut**.

No lo pondría en el documento *Mission & Vision*, porque allí hablamos del propósito de la empresa. Lo incorporaría más adelante, en **Platform_Architecture.md** y posteriormente en **Story_Continuum.md**, donde se define la naturaleza de los universos narrativos.

De hecho, propondría añadir un principio como este:

> **Narrative Chronology is Mutable.**
> The chronology of an authorized story is not fixed. Events may be reordered, branched, merged, skipped, revisited, or rewritten, creating alternative narrative timelines while preserving attribution and complete historical lineage.

Eso abre posibilidades enormes para byDebut:

* historias con líneas temporales alternativas;
* "¿Qué habría pasado si...?" (*What If...?*);
* realidades paralelas;
* precuelas creadas después de la obra original;
* finales alternativos;
* cambios de punto de vista;
* bifurcaciones temporales;
* reconstrucción cronológica por parte del lector;
* múltiples universos coexistiendo con trazabilidad completa.

En otras palabras, **la cronología deja de ser una restricción y pasa a ser una dimensión editable del universo narrativo**. Creo que ese concepto puede convertirse en una de las características más distintivas de byDebut.

# Purpose

This document defines the conceptual architecture of byDebut.

It explains the permanent structure of the platform independently of implementation technologies.

Technologies may evolve.

The architecture should remain stable.

---

# Architectural Philosophy

byDebut is not built around books.

It is built around Living Story Universes.

Every component of the platform exists to support the continuous evolution of authorized stories.

The architecture must enable growth without sacrificing attribution, transparency, or trust.

---

# Core Architectural Layers

The platform is composed of six permanent layers.

## 1. Story Layer

Contains the original intellectual property.

Represents the canonical starting point of every Living Story Universe.

The original work always remains identifiable.

---

## 2. Version Layer

Stores every authorized derivative work.

Each version is independently identifiable.

Versions never overwrite previous versions.

History is immutable.

---

## 3. Relationship Layer

Defines how versions connect.

Relationships may include:

- continuation
- branch
- merge
- alternate ending
- alternate beginning
- parallel universe
- character perspective
- educational adaptation
- cinematic adaptation
- translation
- simplification
- expansion

The relationship graph is permanent.

---

## 4. Attribution Layer

Every contribution records:

- creator
- permissions
- source material
- contribution history
- ownership
- timestamps
- licensing

Attribution can never be removed.

---

## 5. Intelligence Layer

Artificial Intelligence assists users.

AI never becomes the owner of a story.

AI may:

- recommend
- analyze
- summarize
- organize
- generate proposals
- identify inconsistencies
- discover relationships

Final creative authority always belongs to humans.

---

## 6. Ecosystem Layer

The ecosystem connects:

- readers
- authors
- creators
- publishers
- editors
- educators
- researchers
- AI agents

All interactions increase the richness of the Living Story Universe.

---

# Story Evolution Model

Every story evolves through authorized contributions.

No contribution replaces previous work.

Instead, the platform creates an ever-growing graph of creative evolution.

Knowledge accumulates.

History is preserved.

---

# Narrative Time

Chronology is a creative dimension rather than a fixed constraint.

Authorized contributors may:

- reorder events;
- create alternative timelines;
- insert prequels or sequels;
- branch into parallel realities;
- revisit past events;
- merge narrative branches;
- explore hypothetical scenarios.

All temporal modifications must preserve attribution, lineage, and complete version history.

---

# Architectural Principles

The platform must always preserve:

- attribution;
- traceability;
- transparency;
- explainability;
- interoperability;
- scalability;
- modularity;
- extensibility.

---

# Technology Independence

The conceptual architecture is independent of implementation technologies.

Frameworks, databases, AI models, cloud providers, and programming languages may change without altering this architecture.

---

# Relationship to Other Documents

This document defines the conceptual architecture.

The next document, **Product_OS.md**, defines how this architecture is translated into the operational behavior of the platform.
