# CTO Recommendation (A Major Strategic Addition)

While writing this article, one concept became clear enough that I believe it deserves to become a **core pillar of byDebut**.

## Introduce the concept of **Narrative Capital™**

Today, companies talk about:

* Financial Capital
* Human Capital
* Intellectual Capital
* Social Capital

I believe byDebut introduces a new category:

> **Narrative Capital™**

Narrative Capital is the cumulative value created by the evolution of a Living Story Universe over time.

It is composed of multiple dimensions, including:

* **Creative Capital** — originality and quality of contributions.
* **Educational Capital** — value generated for learning.
* **Cultural Capital** — preservation and enrichment of cultural heritage.
* **Community Capital** — engagement, trust, and collaboration.
* **Commercial Capital** — licensing, adaptations, and revenue opportunities.
* **Knowledge Capital** — accumulated insights, annotations, and research.

A Story Passport could eventually display a **Narrative Capital Score**, helping authors, publishers, educators, and investors understand not just how popular a story is, but how much enduring value it has created.

I believe this concept is original, aligns perfectly with the Living Story Economy, and could become one of byDebut's signature intellectual contributions alongside the Story Passport and Living Story Universe concepts.
